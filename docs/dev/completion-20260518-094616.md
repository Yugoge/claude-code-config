1. **allow-1**: Dead `sid = _get_session_id(data)` at `_evaluate_command:658` — assigned, never used
2. **allow-2**: 6 independent per-function `_check_git_allowlist` calls with no structural enforcement at the dispatcher level — silent omission risk for future evaluate-* additions
3. **allow-3**: 6 secondary `check_and_consume_allowlist` calls in bash hook (lines 805, 984, 995, 1007, 1027, 1110) — dead code; global short-circuit at line 615 fires unconditionally before all block rules

5. **allow-5**: IS_SUBAGENT assigned at line 1068 but first needed at line 615 via `check_and_consume_allowlist` — caused duplicate inline parse inside the function and a third inline parse in the `/do` bypass block
6. **allow-6**: 4 independent grant-read implementations — deferred (W1 Won't Have this cycle)
