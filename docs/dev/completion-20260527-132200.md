1. **Step 1.5 (pre-BA Bash hydrator)**: `graphify-query.py` is called directly by the orchestrator via Bash (not a subagent) between Step 1 and Step 2 to extract structural context from the knowledge graph and inject it into BA's dispatch before BA forms its initial interpretation — eliminating BA confirmation bias on ambiguous requirements.
